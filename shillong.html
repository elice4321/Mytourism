Shillong - Scotland of the East

  1. Elephant Falls

    A stunning multi-tiered waterfall near Shillong, perfect for photography and nature walks.

  2. Shillong Peak

    The highest point in Meghalaya offers panoramic views of the entire Shillong city.

  4. Ward's Lake

    A charming artificial lake surrounded by gardens, popular for leisurely walks and paddle boating.

    Ward's Lake

    A museum showcasing the rich cultural heritage of Northeast India through various exhibits.

    Don Bosco Museum

    Known as the "End of Hills," offering breathtaking views of deep gorges and rolling hills.

    Laitlum Canyons

    A beautiful cathedral known for its Gothic architecture and serene ambiance.

    Cathedral of Mary Help of Christians

    The main commercial hub of Shillong, bustling with shops, eateries, and local markets.

    Police Bazaar

    Air Force Museum
